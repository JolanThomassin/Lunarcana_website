S-VI

Jolan Thomassin

S-VI, the spectral medic and sister to Seeve, existed as a silent yet formidable presence within the team. Her ethereal form and enigmatic silence masked a profound strength that resonated through her actions. Unbound by the limitations of mortal existence, she wielded healing powers that transcended the physical realm, offering solace and restoration to her companions in times of dire need. Her silence was not a weakness but rather a testament to her inner fortitude, as she navigated the world with a quiet resolve that spoke volumes about her unwavering dedication to aiding the team.

While her words remained unheard, her healing touch and spectral interventions were undeniable, weaving a tapestry of support and resilience within the group. S-VI's silent strength and mystical abilities became a source of reassurance, a beacon of hope that illuminated the team's darkest moments. Her bond with Seeve, though communicated in whispers between realms, served as an unbreakable tie, showcasing the depth of their connection and the shared commitment to the team's well-being.
