Noxcs

Jolan Thomassin

Noxcs, the fallen angel vying for dominion, emerged as a formidable force fueled by a thirst for power and a chilling ability to harness strength through conquest. Her dark ambitions were as boundless as her malevolent nature, with each conquest empowering her further—a chilling symbiosis between her ruthless actions and her growing might. Noxcs' very essence seemed entwined with the destruction she wrought, her path to governance paved with the lives she extinguished.

Beside her stood Brunhilde, a stalwart companion and Noxcs' right hand—a figure as formidable and unyielding as the fallen angel herself. Together, they forged a path of conquest, annexing territories with calculated precision. Brunhilde's unwavering loyalty and strategic prowess complemented Noxcs' relentless pursuit of power. Their alliance was a symbiotic relationship, each empowering the other in their quest for domination. As they expanded their reach, territory by territory, their malevolent presence cast a shadow over the land, heralding a reign of darkness and conquest.
