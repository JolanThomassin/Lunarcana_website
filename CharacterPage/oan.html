Oan

Jolan Thomassin

Oan, a young samurai adorned with black hair and wrapped in bandages from battles past, led a team of warriors devoted to justice. When rumors surfaced about a tyrannical warlord tormenting a village, Oan and his team embarked on a mission.

Navigating treacherous traps set by the warlord's minions, Oan's leadership guided them through. They arrived at the village, oppressed and fearful, and rallied the villagers to stand against their oppressor.

In a fierce battle, Oan's strategic brilliance turned the tide. Leading by example, he faced the warlord in a decisive duel and emerged victorious, liberating the village.

With hope restored, Oan and his team continued their journey, their unity unbreakable, ready to confront any darkness that threatened the land's peace.
