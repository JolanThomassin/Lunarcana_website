Fhree

Jolan Thomassin

Fhree, Oan's brother, was a silent samurai, embodying strength through actions rather than words. While Oan led with charisma, Fhree's disciplined swordsmanship and unwavering dedication to the samurai code anchored the team. His silent resolve and precise movements in battle spoke volumes, commanding respect and showcasing the essence of samurai virtues. Despite his quiet nature, his loyalty to Oan and the team was unshakable, a steadfast force in their journey.
