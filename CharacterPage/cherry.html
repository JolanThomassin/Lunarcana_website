Cherry

Jolan Thomassin

Cherry, the diminutive yet determined assassin, emerged as a beacon of resilience within the realm. Despite her youth, her prowess in the art of assassination was unmatched. Gifted with agility and cunning, she traversed the shadows, wielding her skills to safeguard the realm against impending threats. Her dedication to protecting the realm mirrored her unspoken love for her older sister, Lillith, the reigning queen.

In the dead of night, Cherry stealthily maneuvered through the realm, thwarting sinister plots and neutralizing adversaries that threatened the kingdom's peace. Her actions were veiled in secrecy, yet her impact resonated throughout the land, a silent guardian dedicated to preserving the realm's harmony. Despite the dangers that lurked in the shadows, Cherry persisted, driven by an unwavering determination to ensure the safety and longevity of her beloved realm.
