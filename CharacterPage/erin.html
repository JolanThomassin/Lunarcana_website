Erin

Jolan Thomassin

Erin, the revered priestess of unparalleled skill within the realm, stood as a beacon of healing and guidance. Her mastery extended beyond mere restoration, as she possessed the remarkable ability to dispel exhaustion, mend wounds, and offer solace through her ethereal slime. Her expertise and compassion made her a revered figure, respected for her unparalleled healing prowess and wisdom.

Erin's presence brought hope and relief to those in need, her abilities not limited to physical ailments but extending to the weariness of the soul. Her soothing slime served as a conduit for her guidance, offering a path of clarity and solace to those seeking direction. Her alliance with Stella, her beloved companion, enhanced their ability to offer both physical and emotional healing, their partnership a testament to the harmony between strength and compassion within the realm. Erin's legacy as the greatest priestess resonated through her ability to restore not just the body but also the spirit, her grace and wisdom leaving an indelible mark on the lives she touched.
