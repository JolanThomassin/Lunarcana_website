Brunhilde

Jolan Thomassin

Brunhilde, the formidable warrior known for her colossal strength and unwavering defense, stood as a bastion of protection within the realm. Her massive arms, honed through countless battles, symbolized not only her physical might but also her determination to shield those under her care. However, even the mightiest are not impervious to wounds of the heart. Brunhilde's indomitable spirit had been scarred by the betrayal of two priestesses, Erin and Stella, whose actions left deep emotional wounds.

Erin and Stella, once trusted allies, had inflicted profound hurt upon Brunhilde, their betrayal cutting deeper than any physical blow. Their treacherous acts had shattered the warrior's trust, casting shadows of doubt upon her once-unshakable faith in others. Despite her imposing stature and impenetrable defense, Brunhilde's heart bore the weight of the pain inflicted by those she had once considered comrades. Her wounds, though not visible to the eye, echoed a vulnerability within her, a testament to the impact of betrayal even upon the mightiest warriors. Yet, despite her pain, Brunhilde remained resolute in her duty, her loyalty to her cause and the protection of those she defended unwavering, a testament to her inner strength amid emotional turmoil.
