Seeve

Jolan Thomassin

Seeve, the ghostly tsundere within the team, was a paradoxical blend of aggression and ethereal enigma. His spectral form crackled with an otherworldly intensity, an aggressive presence that belied a deeper, more guarded demeanor. Though he exuded an aura of aggression, his actions spoke of an underlying care for the team, often cloaked in cryptic and abrupt gestures.

Beneath his fierce exterior lay a hidden tenderness, revealed sporadically through moments of unspoken protection and guidance. His aggressive outbursts, rather than expressions of hostility, often served as a barrier, guarding a vulnerable core that harbored concern for his companions. While his words were sharp and laced with aggression, his actions hinted at a deeper sense of loyalty and a desire to protect those he held dear, even if it meant keeping them at arm's length.

Seeve's tsundere nature, a blend of aggressiveness and unspoken care, added a layer of complexity to the team dynamic. Despite his aggressive demeanor, his underlying intentions were woven with threads of loyalty and a silent dedication to the team's well-being. His journey toward understanding his own emotions, shrouded in cryptic expressions and abrupt gestures, mirrored a delicate balance between guarded protection and unspoken care for his companions.
